# Nabra — Account & Data Deletion Request

We respect your right to control your data. If you would like your **Nabra** account and all associated data permanently deleted, please follow the instructions below.

---

## What gets deleted

When you submit a deletion request, we will permanently remove:

- Your account (Google Sign-In identity stored in Firebase Authentication)
- Your learning progress (levels completed, scores, streak, session history)
- Your problem-letter tracking data
- Your notification preferences
- Any cloud-synced drill/session records associated with your account

**Note:** Audio recordings are **never stored** on our servers. They are sent to Microsoft Azure Speech Services for real-time pronunciation assessment and discarded immediately after scoring — there is nothing to delete on that side.

Anonymized, aggregated analytics (used to improve the app) cannot be tied back to you and are not removed, as they no longer contain personal identifiers.

---

## Timeline

- **Acknowledgement:** within 7 days
- **Full deletion:** within 30 days
- **Confirmation:** you’ll receive an email once deletion is complete

---

## Delete without an account (Guest mode)

If you use Nabra as a **guest** (no sign-in), all your data is stored only on your device. To delete it:

1. Open your device **Settings** → **Apps** → **Nabra** → **Storage** → **Clear data**, or
2. Simply **uninstall** the app

No deletion request is needed because nothing is stored on our servers.
